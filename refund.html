Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2026

📦 Our Commitment

At Adox Foods, we are committed to delivering quality foodstuff to every student. If something goes wrong with your order, we will do our best to make it right.

✅ When You Qualify for a Refund

You are eligible for a refund or replacement if: you received the wrong item, the item delivered was damaged or expired, or your order was confirmed and paid for but never delivered within 2 hours with no communication from our team.

❌ When Refunds Don't Apply

Refunds will not be issued if you ordered the wrong item, changed your mind after payment, or provided an incorrect delivery location. All sales are final once items have been correctly delivered.

🔄 How to Request a Refund

Contact us on WhatsApp at +234 801 234 5678 within 1 hour of receiving your order. Include your Order ID, a description of the issue, and a photo if applicable. We will respond within 30 minutes during business hours.

💸 Refund Method

Approved refunds will be processed back to your bank account within 24 hours, or you may choose to receive a store credit for your next order.

For any issues or complaints, we are always reachable on WhatsApp. We value every customer and will always treat you fairly. 🧡
